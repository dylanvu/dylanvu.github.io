About
Former chemical engineering student from the University of California, Santa Barbara turned computer science student at the University of California, Irvine.
I mostly program using NodeJS and TypeScript, but I'm also familiar with Python and C++. I do know a bit on Flutter and Dart, a little bit of C# in the context of Unity. I also some MATLAB from my background in Chemical Engineering.

I think of them as the best way to learn since you're committing to finishing a mini project, it's quite short term, and it's a great chance to challenge yourself and step outside of what you already know. I attribute the majority of my development in this field just to hackathons.
